## About The Project

[![Product Name Screen Shot][product-screenshot]](product-link)
The pig game! this project was a lot of fun to develop and I really enjoy it! The way the game works is as follows: 2 players take turns rolling the dice, they are allowed to roll the dice as many times and they would like to & each time the dice is rolled the dice value will be added to the players current score. Once the player rolls a 1 on the dice then all of their current score will be LOST and it will be the next players turn! In order for a player to keep their current score they must press the "HOLD" button! this will transfer the active players current score into their total score (bank). Points in a players bank CANNOT be lost and are 100% safe! A player can win the game but accumulating 100 total points in their points bank!
